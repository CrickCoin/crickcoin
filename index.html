Community-Driven Governance

CrickCoin is governed by fans, for fans. Through decentralized voting, locked treasury, and open collaboration, we ensure every voice counts.

Voting

All major decisions are decided by the community – no single point of control. Every holder has a voice and vote in shaping CrickCoin’s future.

Treasury

1200M tokens locked in UniCrypt. Released only by majority consensus. Funds can be allocated to marketing, partnerships, or community rewards based on open proposals.

Decentralization

No central authority. Cricket lovers worldwide collectively control CrickCoin’s direction, ensuring a fair and inclusive environment.

Community Initiatives

Fans propose new ideas, events, and projects—such as sponsoring local cricket tournaments. The best initiatives are approved through transparent community polls.
